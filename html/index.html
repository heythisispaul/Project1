What we do!  (and why you'll love us.)

ShelterPointer connects you to your next best friend. We provide a simple and clean search function to show you current, adoptable, animals in your local shelters and rescues. In addition, if you sign up with us, we will provide you updates when the type of pet you're looking for becomes available. Stay connected with the people that matter.

About ShelterPointer

Pet connect was built with MDBootstrap and finds pets using Petfinder.com's API for searches. Maps are provided by Google.
